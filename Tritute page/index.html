Lionel Messi

"You have to fight to reach your dream. You have to sacrifice and work hard for it."

Below a time line of Lionel Messi's life:

  • 1987 - Born in Rosario, Argentina to Celia Maria Cuccittini and Jorge Messi
  • 1994 - Messi joined the league of Newell Old Boys
  • 2000 - Messi was diagnosed with a growth defect and couldn't afford treatment
  • 2000 - Lionel Messi received a trial run at the FC Barcelona, he also moved to Spain to get treatment paid for by the coach of FC Barcelona
  • 2003 - Made his first appearance on field against FC Porto in a friendly match
  • 2005 - Became the youngest player to score a goal for the FC Barcelona team
  • 2007 - Created the Leo Messi Foundation to help support disadvantaged children
  • 2009 - He won his first Ballon d'Or at the age of 22
  • 2010 - Messi won the award, his second consecutive Ballon d'Or victory
  • 2011 - Messi, who plays for Barcelona, won the inaugural Ballon d'Or in 2010 and went on to win three in a row
  • 2012 - Messi's wife gave birth to a son
  • 2012 - Lionel Messi has won the FIFA Ballon d'Or for an unprecedented fourth time in a row
  • 2015 - Messi's wife gave birth to their second son
  • 2015 - Messi wins record fifth FIFA Ballon d'Or
  • 2017 - Got married to Antonella Roccuzzo
  • 2018 - Lionel Messi and Antonella Roccuzzo are set to have their third son together and the Barcelona star has revealed he will be called Ciro
  • 2019 - Lionel Messi became the first men's footballer to win the Ballon d'Or for a sixth time
